Assistive Wearables For Everyday Use We are developing an assistive wearable device that seamlessly integrates with everyday accessories(beginning with glasses and rings) to make them smart, efficient, and genuinely helpful. Designed for universal accessibility, the device effortlessly clips onto personal wearables, enhancing them with intelligent assistive functions that adapt to each user’s specific needs. Its modular and customizable design allows individuals to select features relevant to their lifestyle or disability, while maintaining comfort, aesthetics, and ease of use. By connecting multiple modules within a single ecosystem, this innovation transforms ordinary accessories into personalized assistive companions—empowering users with independence, inclusivity, and smart support in their daily lives.
