Smart Visual Technology This project explores the need for wearable smart glasses designed for everyday use by both individuals with disabilities and the general population. It investigates how smart glasses can enhance routine activities through hands-free interaction, real-time information access, and context-aware support. The study includes a competitive analysis of existing smart glass technologies and examines current design, functionality, and user experience trends in the industry. The goal is to identify opportunities for improvement and innovation in the development of inclusive, multifunctional smart eyewear.
